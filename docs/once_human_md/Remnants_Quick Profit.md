Summary[]
Quick Profit is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide. 

Location[]
Quick Profit can be found inside the house at 6390,-5116 in Throughville, Dayton Wetlands. It is collected as part of the task Main Story: Welcome Back.

Quick Profit[]
Throughville, Middle Dayton Wetlands
Hey, old pals from the convoy!
A scout has reported that there's a mysterious Deviation at Rotten Manor. A big buyer from the black market is offering triple the price to get their hands on it.  This is a rare opportunity! This money could fund your convoy's trips for a while. Once one get the Deviation, you can settle up with me directly.
