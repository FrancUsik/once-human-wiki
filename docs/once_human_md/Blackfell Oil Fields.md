Summary[]

Points of Interest[]
Rift Anchor[]
The Rift Anchors in Blackfell Oil Fields are located at 5282,-1413 and 5510,-1343. 
NOTE: One of the Rift Anchors has a Morphic Crate - Gear next to it, it will attack when Players get to close.
