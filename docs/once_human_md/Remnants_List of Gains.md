Summary[]
List of Gains is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
List of Gains can be found inside the building at 5552,-4796 in Cliffside Camp, Dayton Wetlands.

List of Gains[]
The "Deboner" Earl, Marauder
Cliffside Camp, West Dayton Wetlands
March 8, ▮▮26
Early June - Bleak. 146 pounds of fresh meat, lots of it bones; 1 pair of jeans1 1 checked girl's shirt1 1 crappy backpack no one else wants1 1M29 with a broken handle, no ammunition. I don't know how she's still alive.
Mid June - Slim pickings out on the road. Nearly starved to death. Will suggested a raid on Citrus County, and everyone agreed. Failed to fight our way in, Robert and Leanne killed, everyone recovered. Will was rescued, but wounded, and the group voted to help him responsible for the failure of the attack. That's 414.5 pounds for the three of us, so we'll be living it up until the end of the month.
Late June - A bumper harvest! 1 FAL and 115 rounds of 7.62 caliber ammunition, 2 sets of adult clothes, 2 sets of children's clothes; 566 pounds of mean, juicy and fresh. There was too much food, I had to get myself some salt water and get my curing game on.
