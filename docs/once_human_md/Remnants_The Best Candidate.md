Summary[]
The Best Candidate is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
The Best Candidate can be found on the porch of the house at 4966,-5697 in Ridge Transfer Station, Broken Delta.

The Best Candidate[]
Denise Cooper, Union Explorer
Ridge Transfer Station, North Broken Delta
October 9, ▮▮33
Dear Trent:
On my journey back, I received the devastating news of Mayor Dave Joyce's passing and I am deeply saddened by this. He was a loyal comrade, and though our time working together was short, I know that this is a great loss for Meyer's Market and the entire Union.
Although you seem reluctant to take on more administrative duties, I must speak plainly: at present, you are Meyer's Market's most dependable individual. Therefore, Ted and I would be greatly relieved if you were willing to consider running for the position of the new mayor.
All seems well with the maintenance status of the Ridge Transfer Station. It seems the convoys have fulfilled their obligations to maintain the station during their recent trips between Meyer's Market and the north.
Things are going smoothly on my end, and I should arrive at Tall Grass as planned. I will keep a close eye on the group that you mentioned. If they indeed originate from the west as you suspected, then we must be mindful that they may already have infiltrated Tall Grass.
I wish us both the best.
