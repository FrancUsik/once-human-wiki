An Unexpected Catch[]
John Braun, Marauder
Aiden's Auto Repair, Northeast Dayton Wetlands
February 15, ▮▮22
Guess what I caught this morning? That little brat Nick Collins!
Oh yes, the  one and only Nick Collins, that Aiden "Hinge" Collins' sickly nephew. Can you believe it? Turns out, the Collins family never left after all!
How could we all be so damn stupid? That Aiden Collins is a survival obsessive. Would he really just abandoned his home and head for the hills when things start to get a little rough?! No way. I reckon the family built themselves a hidden shelter nearby and piled it high with all the supplies they've accumulated over the years...
Anyway, Nick Collins has had the shit kicked out of him, but he still won't talk. Reeve has given orders to hang the kid from the wind turbine tower and leave him there.
"Hinge" is sure to show up here before long. I just hope it's not while I'm on duty.
