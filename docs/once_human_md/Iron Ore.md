Obtaining[]
Iron Ore is obtained from mining Iron Ore nodes in the wilderness, these nodes have a rusty colored pattern, and are commonly found in level 20+ regions, such as the Iron River region in southeast Nalcott. Mining Iron Ore requires, at a minimum, a Bronze Pickaxe. Players can use higher tier tools, such as Steel Pickaxes, Alloy Pickaxes, Tungsten Alloy Pickaxes, or Drills, which is increase the yield of ore they obtain.
Although Iron Ore is not readily available at lower levels, players that have The Digby Boy deviation can obtain Iron Ore, even in zones which do not have Iron Ore nodes

Uses[]
Iron Ore in its raw form can be smelted into Steel Ingots.
