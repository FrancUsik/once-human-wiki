Summary[]
Screw Keefer is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Screw Keefer can be found on a box at 7255,-5365 in Sutherland Family Orchard, Dayton Wetlands.

Screw Keefer[]
Anthony Hammer, Accountant
Sutherland Vineyard, South Dayton Wetlands
March 11, ▮▮19
Screw Keefer. Screw that no-good sonofabitch Keefer Sutherland!!
He really doesn't care about besides himself at this point. Never mind his campaign of terror out on the roads and at the docks, now he's started bullying us too. Yesterday Cleo send some goods to Citrus County, and the guy deducted 25%! He said that included the Old Man's regular fee, plus a cut for that lot in uniform. So, what about the money we sent over at the beginning of the month, huh? Did he use it to wipe his ass?
If Ms. Anne doesn't do something soon - but wait. That's not really a solution either, is it? The whole family has been hollowed out by him and Nelson. Anne and that lot haven't got a hope in hell. And I like our chances even less.
Before long, we'll all be lining up to lick the old man's boots. And so be it.
