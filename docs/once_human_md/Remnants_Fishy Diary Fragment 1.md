Summary[]
Fishy Diary Fragment 1 is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide. It is only available during the task Legacy of Bloodbeak.

Location[]
Fishy Diary Fragment 1 can be found by looting the unconscious Vulture in Coastside Plaza, Dayton Wetlands at 7428,-4902 .

Fishy Diary Fragment 1[]
The "Ninja" Daisy
The South of Coastside Plaza
I can't believe I, father! I MADE IT! To the place where your great cause was born! My heart is filled with gratitude and awe for the foundation you laid for us Vultures today! They should all come here and pay their homage to you and every drop of blood you shed!
From your precious notes, I learned that you left behind some valuable items here. I didn't come to steal your legacy for my own gain! No, I want these items to serve as a token of our great accomplishments. I will show them to the other Vultures, so they can learn about Reeve Ricci the Bloodbeak, our great founding father!
