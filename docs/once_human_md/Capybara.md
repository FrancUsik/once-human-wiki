Summary[]
Capybara are passive animals that can be slain for Beast Meat, or captured for animal breeding. 

Description[]
Locations[]
The best location to find Capybara is Mushroom Cave, Broken Delta.

How to Capture[]
[See: Ranching]

Taming[]
Once a capybara has been captured, Players can place them inside their territory, along with an animal trough that has food and water inside it. Food and water may also be discarded on the ground. Capybara have a diet of Fruit, Vegetables, Grains and fodder; their favorite foods consist of herbs, such as Sage and Honeysuckle.
Tameness increases as the animal's hydration and hunger meters increase above 30%. Their tameness will decrease if their hydration and hunger meters reach 0%.
Capybara do not give off any byproducts.
If slaughtered, capybara will provide the Player with Beast Meat and Hide.
