Summary[]
Animals in the Shop is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Animals in the Shop can be found on the counter of the grocery store at 5700,-4512 in Dayton Hospital, Dayton Wetlands.

Animals in the Shop[]
Owen Hicks, Grocery Store Owner, Former Agent Team Leader
Dayton Hospital, North Dayton Wetlands
February 9, ▮▮19
Of course. I understand. Your men are nervous and exhausted. After the sudden accident in the Project BL082W construction zone, you were all on duty for fifteen hours straight. And after all that, you just want to rest and let off some steam.
But, is any of this a valid reason for these five men to come charging into my warehouse, and harass that poor girl?
I call you Captain when I want to be polite, and Tommy when I'm in a good mood, but right now, Tom Ulrich, I'm giving you two choices. Option 1: You bring those animals here in handcuffs yourself after I shut up shop this evening, and let me deal with them. Don't worry, I'll let them live... Hmm. And I won't cut their nuts off either... Option2" You sit there and wait for a summons from the Security Court.
And one last thing. I think you already know what I did for Rosetta before I took over this grocery store.
