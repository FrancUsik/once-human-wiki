Summary[]
Well-Hidden Diary is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide. It is only available during the task Legacy of Bloodbeak.

Location[]
Well-Hidden Diary can be found by looting the final crate in Legacy of Bloodbeak.

Well-Hidden Diary[]
Coastside Plaza
Okay, that's the food and medicine hidden! Now to find another spot to stash the weapons and ammo. After all, I'd be a damn fool to leave everything all in one place.
Aha, that room on the second floor of the clinic! It's empty, and definitely forgotten after that business with Kelly Hill.
