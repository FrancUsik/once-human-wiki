Summary[]
The Primary Stove is the first tier of stove in-game, and allows the player to craft consumables. 

Obtaining[]
The memetic for the Primary Stove can be unlocked via the Cradle under the Logistics page, 01 Awakening. The memetic, Stoves, costs 1 cipher and  0body[data-theme="light"] .mw-parser-output .energy-links-icon{filter:drop-shadow(0 0 0.125em black)}.

Building[]
To build a Primary Stove, enter Build Mode. The Primary Stove can be found under Facilities &gt; Crafting Process.
