Summary[]
Mastering basic disassembly techniques is essential for survival in the wilderness.

How To Get[]
The memetic for the Disassembly Bench is automatically unlocked when Players first enter the world. The memetic node, Disassembly Techniques, can be found in the Cradle under the Infrastructure  page, 01 Awakening.
