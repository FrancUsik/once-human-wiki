Summary[]
Haunted Hospital is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Haunted Hospital can be found on the hospital reception desk at 5811,-4460 in Dayton Hospital, Dayton Wetlands.

Haunted Hospital[]
Upton Teresa, Local Resident
Dayton Hospital, North Dayton Wetlands
December 24, ▮▮18
The hospital is haunted! I'm not making this up. Two days ago, when I was delivering meals on the balcony corridor on the second floor, I saw a box that wasn't supposed to be there. When I approached, the box vanished! I also saw an empty wheelchair parked in the deserted hallway. There was no one on it, yet it started turning and slowly rolled towards me! I was terrified, but it was definitely not an illusion. Later, Rosetta's security guards came and vehemently denied any abnormal occurrences in the hospital. They made me promise not to spread rumors. But doesn't that mean something's not right? Anyway, there's no way I'm going to that hospital again, no matter how big the order from Rosetta is.
