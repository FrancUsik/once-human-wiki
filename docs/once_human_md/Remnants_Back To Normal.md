Summary[]
Back To Normal is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Back To Normal can be found inside the same building as Paul (Dayton Hospital) at 5655,-4643 in Dayton Warehouse, Dayton Wetlands.

Back To Normal[]
Kaitlin March, Mayfly
Dayton Warehouse, North Dayton Wetlands
September 20, ▮▮37
This place once harbored a Deviation, or rather, this place was a Deviation itself for sure. However, the effects of certain unknown entities have dissipated, like gravel blown away by the relentless winds of time. Even though there may be faint traces lingering in some crevices, they no longer pose any threat.
It's unclear how Rosetta contained this place; they left behind some documentation, but the information is scarce... I can't even be sure if Rosetta restored this place to its original state of if the Deviation naturally faded away on its own.
At least, there are still plenty of useful supplies here (thank god for this place's unfavorable reputation in the past). I can't possibly carry everything on my own, so I'll notify the pioneers from the Union later. They are building a shelter called Deadsville and could use things here. It's a perfect opportunity to negotiate some benefits from them as well.
