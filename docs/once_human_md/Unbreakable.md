How To Get[]
Securement Silo - EX1 in Hard and Pro Modes.
Defeat the Forsaken Giant.
Complete the LEA Research Lab.
Win the Prime War in Easy, Normal, Hard, or Nightmare Mode.
Core Effect[]
Fortress Warfare Range -30%; Attack +15% when in Heavy Duty status.

Suffixes[]
Possible Suffixes include Violent, Precision, and Fortress Warfare.
The Unbreakable Mods obtained in dungeons in Pro Mode always have Violent or Precision as their Suffixes.
The Unbreakable Explosion Mods obtained in the Prime War always have Fortress Warfare as their Suffixes.

AWS.338 - Black Panther
