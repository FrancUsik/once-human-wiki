Summary[]
The Holy Place is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
The Holy Place can be found inside the control in the bunker at 5039,-5398 in Abandoned Hideout, Broken Delta. The bunker is only available during Murmurs in the Forest.

The Holy Place[]
White King, Priest
Abandoned Hideout, North Broken Delta
March 26, ▮▮39
We found a holy place. My Lord has never led me astray. The omens in that dream are coming true, one by one!
The world sees this place as a dumping ground, but they don't know it's actually a cornucopia of blessings. I see a giant tree, about to break out of the soil! Within the trunk breeds a fetus that will never be born or awaken. This is the very proof of my Lord's mercy. My almighty Stardust, you showed mercy on that poor mother and her weak child, granting them a life force that never fades and serenity that will last for all eternity.
