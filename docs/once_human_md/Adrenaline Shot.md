Did you mean: Rift Adrenaline Shot
Adrenaline Shots are an essential consumable to carry when out exploring the wilderness. Sudden or strong attacks can knock the player down to their knees, giving them 60 seconds to be revived, but when carrying Adrenaline Shots the player can revive themselves. There is a 60 seconds cool down once the player has revived themselves, causing them to wait if they go down again within 60 seconds of being revived.
The player must first unlock the memetic node: Adrenaline Shots (2 cipher,  0) in the Crafting section of the memetics page, granted they have unlocked the prerequisite node: Essential Tools.

Crafting[]
Adrenaline Shots can occasionally be found in crates, however players may find it more useful to craft them. They can be crafted at any Supplies Workbench.
