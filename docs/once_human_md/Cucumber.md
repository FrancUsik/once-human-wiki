Obtaining[]
Cucumbers can not be gathered from the wilderness, but can still be grown by planting Cucumber Seeds. Cucumber seeds can be found by random chance when gathering player grown Cucumbers, or from the Fetch-A-Lot Bunny, if this deviation is placed inside an Isolated Securement Unit in the player's territory. Additionally, cucumber seeds can be purchased from Zhao Jintan (5758,-6546) in Meyer's Market for  1,000.

Planting[]
Cucumber Seeds can be planted in Loamy Soil or Planter Boxes, they take 6 hours to mature and need an irrigation level between 50 - 90, and a light level between 70 - 100. For more information on how to cultivate this plant, see the page Seeds and Agriculture.
