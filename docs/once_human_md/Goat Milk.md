How To Get[]
Goat Milk can be harvested from slain animals of the goat variety (Ram and Ewe), or collected as a byproduct of Ewes once tamed.

Tips[]
Although there are no ill-effects to consuming unprocessed Goat Milk, it is more valuable once processed into something else such as Butter (which is required to craft legendary foods such as Safety Sandwich.)
