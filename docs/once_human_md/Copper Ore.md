Obtaining[]
Copper Ore is obtained from mining Copper Ore nodes in the wilderness, these nodes have smaller golden colored nodes extruding from the rock, and are found in all regions. Mining Copper Ore does not require the player to have a tool. However, players can use higher tier tools, such as Crude Pickaxes, Bronze Pickaxes, Steel Pickaxes, Alloy Pickaxes, Tungsten Alloy Pickaxes, or Drills, to increase the yield of ore they obtain.
Players that have The Digby Boy deviation can obtain Copper Ore.

Uses[]
Copper Ore is used in crafting Furnaces and Primary Supplies Workbenches, and in its raw form can be smelted into Copper Ingots.
