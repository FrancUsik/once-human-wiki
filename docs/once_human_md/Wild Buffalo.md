Taming[]
Buffaloes have a diet of Grains and fodder, and usually have favorite foods such as sage and honeysuckle. Once they have been captured they are ready to be placed down inside the territory and given food and water, by either discarding the items onto the ground, or by placing an animal trough and placing the food and water inside of it.
Tameness will increase if the animal's hydration and hunger metres are above 30%, or by feeding the animal their favorite foods. Beware, as tameness will decrease over time if the animal's hydration or hunger reaches 0%.
Once tamed Buffaloes will provide the player with Milk. To gather these items you must select the animal by interacting with it, and then press 'G' to "Harvest Byproduct". Buffalo also produce Wild Buffalo Organic Fertilizer, which can be used as heating fuel at a campfire, or turned into Fertilizer in a Compost Bin.
Note: If animals are hungry players will not be able to harvest their byproducts.
For more information on byproducts, see article: Ranching
If slaughtered with high tameness, Wild Buffalo will provide the player with Buffalo Meat and Rawhide.
