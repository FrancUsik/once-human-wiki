# Boar

Boars are hostile animals that can be slain for Pork, or captured for animal breeding. Boars do not give the player byproducts. For more information on byproducts, see article: Ranching

Taming[]
Boars have a diet of Grains, fodder, Vegetables, Fruits, and Meat, and usually have favorite foods such as apples and oranges. Once they have been captured they are ready to be placed down inside the territory and given food and water, by either discarding the items onto the ground, or by placing an animal trough and placing the food and water inside of it.
Tameness will increase if the animal's hydration and hunger metres are above 30%, or by feeding the animal their favorite foods. Beware, as tameness will decrease over time if the animal's hydration or hunger reaches 0%.
Once tamed Boars will also protect the player's territory against enemies.
If slaughtered with high tameness they are likely to drop Bristles and Pork.
