Summary[]
Breanna's Prayer Book is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Breanna's Prayer Book can be found inside the bunker at 5033,-5414 in Abandoned Hideout, Broken Delta. It is collected as a part of the Main Story task Murmurs in the Forest.

Breanna's Prayer Book[]
Breanna
Abandoned Hideout, North Broken Delta
January 8, ▮▮39
...I questioned you, for I once feared loss...
Now I have nothing left but this frail, dying body... and the one in my womb who shares my fate...
Peace and immortality... I was promised these in your name...
Now I regret it.
