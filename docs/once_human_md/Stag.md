Summary[]
Stag are passive animals that can be slain for Deer Meat, or captured for animal breeding with it's female counterpart the Doe.

Description[]
Male deer have large, branched antlers that show off their leadership and authority.

Locations[]
Stag are extremely common in the Dayton Wetlands and Chalk Peak.

How to Capture[]
[See: Ranching]

Taming[]
Once a ram has been captured, Players can place them inside their territory, along with an animal trough that has food and water inside it. Food and water may also be discarded on the ground. Rams have a diet of Fruit, Vegetables, Grains, and fodder; their favorite foods consist of Wheat and Corn.
Tameness increases as the animal's hydration and hunger meters increase above 30%. Their tameness will decrease if their hydration and hunger meters reach 0%.
Once tamed, rams will product byproduct of N/A. To gather, interact with the animal and press [G] to "Harvest Byproduct." Byproduct can be harvested every 16 hours. If an animal becomes hungry, byproducts will become unavailable.
If slaughtered, rabbits will provide the Player with Deer Meat and Deer Hide.
