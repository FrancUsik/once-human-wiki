Taming[]
Ewe's have a diet of Grains and fodder, and usually have favorite foods such as sage and honeysuckle. Once they have been captured they are ready to be placed down inside the territory and given food and water, by either discarding the items onto the ground, or by placing an animal trough and placing the food and water inside of it.
Tameness will increase if the animal's hydration and hunger metres are above 30%, or by feeding the animal their favorite foods. Beware, as tameness will decrease over time if the animal's hydration or hunger reaches 0%.
Once tamed, they will provide the player with Wool and Goat Milk. To gather these items you must select the animal by interacting with it, and then press 'G' to "Harvest Byproduct". For additional information on animal byproducts see: Ranching
If slaughtered Ewes will provide the player with Goat Meat and Goat Milk.
Note: If animals are hungry players will not be able to harvest their byproducts.Note: Ewes byproducts are able to be harvested every 8 hours.
