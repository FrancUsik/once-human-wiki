How To Use[]
Not to be confused with Water Pump (Production Processing), this pump is used to move water from a low point to a higher point.
The "inputs" are containers that sit at a point lower than the pump.
The "outputs" are containers that sit at a point higher than the pump.
The pump can have multiple inputs and multiple outputs.
The pump will move approximately one unit per second in a round-robin fashion. Buffers/Tanks are usually placed on the output side of the pump and not the input side since the pump outpaces most production facilities.
