Summary[]
Wait Until I Get Back is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Wait Until I Get Back can be found on the second floor of the building at 5082,-6186 in Sutherland Chemical Plant, Broken Delta.

Wait Until I Get Back[]
Dennis Mobley, Rig Team captain
Sutherland Chemical Plant, North Broken Delta
June 20, ▮▮19
How's the crude oil looking this month? Does it meet quality control standards? What is Donald Sutherland playing at? Who's he trying to kid?
The question isn't whether we've got a problem, but whether it's a big problem, or a REALLY big problem! Just look at this stuff! The oil has turned blue!!! My old man spent forty years on oil rigs out in jungles and deserts. His old man was out drilling in the icy seas of the Arctic before him. And I've been hopping from posting to posting on different oil rigs ever since I was 18. So, it's safe to say I know a thing or two about oil, and this is definitely the freakiest thing I've ever seen.
Tell your people to shut down all the oil wells, now!!! Don't wait. No matter what that idiot Donal Sutherland says. The Sutherlands might be able to pull this crap over in the Wetlands, but not here.
Tell everyone to stay as far away from the wells as possible. Don't even try and take samples. No one can say  for sure whether or not that stuff is dangerous. I'm going to headquarters to get help. Don't do anything else until I get back.
