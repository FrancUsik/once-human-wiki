Summary[]
Reckless Bomber is a Unstable Bomber status Weapon Mod.

How To Get[]
Open Weapon Crates in the Red Sands or Blackheart Region.
Defeat the Shadow Hound in Hard or Pro Mode.
Complete the LEA Research Lab.
Win the Prime War in Easy, Normal, Hard, or Nightmare Mode.
Core Effect[]
For every 1% Crit Rate, Unstable Bomber DMG +0.5%.

Attribute Effects[]
Attribute effects are randomized for each mod.

Suffixes[]
Possible Suffixes include Violent, Precision, Deviant Energy, and Unstable Bomber.
The Heavy Explosives Mods obtained in dungeons in Pro Mode always have Violent, Precision, or Deviant Energy as their Suffixes.
The Heavy Explosives Mods obtained in the Prime War always have Unstable Bomber as their Suffixes.
