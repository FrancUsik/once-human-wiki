Fix the Boat[]
Harris Blanken, Wharf Manager
Harborside, Southeast Broken Delta
July 31, ▮▮19
That sudden wave bashed the Fishbone against the rocks. It'll take time and resources to fix it, and we don't know how much time we have left. We're too busy fighting off those monsters in town. We an only spare a couple of the elderly and kids to try and fix it. It's the best we can do right now.
And that Dr. Hayden, from Rosetta, has skipped town for some reason. He's very useful, but still, I can't say I'm sad to see the back of him. These monsters are coming from the Monolith. Ultimately, the blame for all this rests with him. Justin and Mary have been protecting him. Maybe, once we can finally set sail, I'll get the chance to discretely push him overboard...
