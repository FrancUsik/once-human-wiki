Crude Pickaxe is the first tier of pickaxe, and unlock the ability for Players to mine more efficiently allowing the player to mine nodes faster and gather more materials when doing so.

How To Get[]
The recipe for Crude Pickaxe is automatically unlocked when Players first enter the world. 

How To Craft[]
Crude Pickax can be crafted at a Supplies Workbench.
