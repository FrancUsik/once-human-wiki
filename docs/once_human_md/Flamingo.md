Taming[]
Flamingos have a diet of Fish, Shellfish, and seaweed, and usually have favorite foods such as Hawthorn berries and Tomatoes. Once they have been captured they are ready to be placed down inside the territory and given food and water, by either discarding the items onto the ground, or by placing an animal trough and placing the food and water inside of it.
Tameness will increase if the animal's hydration and hunger metres are above 30%, or by feeding the animal their favorite foods. Beware, as tameness will decrease over time if the animal's hydration or hunger reaches 0%.
Once tamed, Flamingos will provide the player with Feathers and Bird Eggs. To gather these items you must select the animal by interacting with it, and then press 'G' to "Harvest Byproduct". For additional information on animal byproducts see: Ranching
If slaughtered with high tameness Flamingos will likely provide the player with Poultry and Feathers.
Note: If animals are hungry players will not be able to harvest their byproducts.
