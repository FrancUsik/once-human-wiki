Summary[]
Desperate Hostility is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Desperate Hostility can be found on the second floor of the Stardust Monitoring Station at 7564,-4806, outside of Coastside Plaza, Dayton Wetlands.

Desperate Hostility[]
Hogan Hausman, Rosetta Researcher
Stardust Monitoring Station, South Dayton Wetlands
July 22, ▮▮19
All comms have been cut off, and the emergency shelter regulation stated in the safety manual are all useless gibberish! We are now trapped with some surviving locals. The monitoring station has been overrun, and most of the researchers couldn't escape. The Great Ones are feasting on a frenzy, and soon they will expand their predatory range. If we don't escape... But those locals don't trust us; their eyes are filled with hostility. I understand they believe Rosetta is responsible for all this, and it might very well be true. However, I'm just a low-level researcher, I know nothing about those internal affairs! We must make the understand that now is not the time for infighting...
