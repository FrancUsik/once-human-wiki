Iridium Crystal is a crafting material in Once Human.

How To Get[]
Iridium Crystal can be obtained by looting settlements between levels 31-40, which are found within the Chalk Peak region. It can also be looted from The Wanderer.
The following locations within Chalk Peak are recommended when for farming Iridium Crystal:

Holt Town
Furnace Lair
Gaia Military Base
Evergreen Vineyard
Rotten Saddle
Monolith of Thirst
Alpha Institute
Silvershore Resort
How To Use[]
Iridium Crystal is a primarily material used f upgrading and repairing weapons, as well as a key material for crafting Tier IV Weapons, which unlock at level 30.
