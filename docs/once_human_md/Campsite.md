# Campsite

A moveable spawnpoint, crafted from Logs and Gravel. Can be placed down by pressing 'T'. Once the Campsite has been placed it begins a 24 hour timer after which the campsite will be removed and will have to be replaced.

Uses[]
The Campsite will protect the player the same as them being inside of their territory, causing reduced energy and hydration depletion by 50%.
You can also use the campsite's built in workbench to craft food, the Rustic Hat, Rustic Jacket, Rustic Pants, Rustic Shoes, and Rustic Gloves; as well as the RUSTIC CROSSBOW B.P., and arrows to go with it.

Way of Winter[]
During the Way of Winter scenario, the campsite protects the player the same as them being inside of their territory, causing reduced energy and hydration depletion by 50%, and ensuring that hypothermia and heatstroke do not being severe.
Additionally to the campsite's built in workbench, the flaming barrel to the right of the campsite will increase the players body temperature by 10 degrees.
