Ain't Got Squat can be found inside the grocery store at 6702,-5716 in Overlook Town, Dayton Wetlands.

Ain't Got Squat[]
The "Baldy" Matt, Marauder
Overlook Town, South Dayton Wetlands
November 5, ▮▮35
This place ain't got squat. That gormless prick Glen has never down anything right in his whole lie, and of course this time was no different. And I trusted him! I was ready to risk my neck to explore this Deviant-infested beach!
Maybe those Sutherland gangsters used to smuggle arms, or some other rate goods. I'm sure they've got themselves a few secret storerooms hidden around the place. Forgotten for decades after the Starfall, stuffed full of riches... But we ain't finding none of those storerooms here, that's for sure! There's nothing but piles of moldy junk here of this crappy dock!
The Sutherland family is supposed to be from Overlook Town originally - of wait. Nope, it was Mola Island. That beautiful villa has long since fallen into ruin, but there are sure to be some goodies hidden in the rubble... Of course, you'd have to be nuts to actually go in there. The place is far too dangerous.
