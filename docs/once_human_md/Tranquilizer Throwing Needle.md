How To Get[]
The recipe for Tranquilizer Throwing Needle is unlock after purchasing the Animal Capture memetic in the Cradle, under the Logistics page, 04 Transcendence.
For Players with the Beastmaster class, the recipe for Tranquilizer Throwing Needle automatically unlocked when Players first enter the world.

How To Craft[]
Tranquilizer Throwing Needle can be crafted at a Supplies Workbench.
