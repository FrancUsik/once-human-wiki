Meta's will want to get their hands on some of these ingots to ensure that they are prepared for their hardening adventures.

How To Get[]
Aluminum Ingots are mainly produced by smelting x5 Aluminum Ore and x1 Acid in an Electric Furnace. This can only be done once the scenario has reached Phase 2: Tame the Staroid, as the memetic node is located in the 4th tab on the Infrastructure page titled: Aluminum Ingots (5 Cipher and  700).
Aluminum ore can be found naturally out in the wilderness in level 30+ areas, or can be collected by the The Digby Boy Deviation, if there is a powered Mining Operation Platform within the same territory as the Deviation's Isolated Securement Unit.

Related Specilizations[]
If the player has an electric furnace built with the Memetic Specialization: Electric Furnace: Electrolysis, crafting an Aluminum, or Tungsten Ingot, will yield two ingots for the same material cost. This can be utilised to save on Acid costs or to maximse profits when selling to Traders and/or other players.
