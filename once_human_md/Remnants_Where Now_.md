Where Now?[]
Brad Lito, Brewer
High Banks Nest, North Broken Delta
May 2, ▮▮30
It's unbelievable... Mr. Owen is dead. Killed by Rosetta - I saw it with my very own eyes. They pretended they were helping us. All that crap about "aid" and "our common humanity." They said they would help us keep our shelter - help us keep o living. It makes me sick to my stomach.
But where do I go now... Why did I run out of there, like a crazy person...? I guess I could head for the Railway Junction but if I did that, I'd have to live my like under the heel of those Rosetta goons. I'd rather die than live like that. Maybe I could head south and join the Marauders? I think they'd take in someone like me. I can brew, and I know how to run a bar... But that seems risky. If I just can make it a little further down the river and find Simon Meyer's gang...
I hope they remember me. But, God, I hope they've forgotten the time Owen drove them all out.+
