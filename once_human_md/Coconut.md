Obtaining[]
Coconuts can be gathered from the wilderness, but can not be grown by planting seeds. Coconuts can be found by random chance from the Fetch-A-Lot Bunny, if this deviation is placed inside an Isolated Securement Unit in the player's territory.
Coconuts cannot be found in The Way of Winter scenario; the only way to obtain them is felling trees using a Silver Pickaxe. Felling trees with silver pickaxes yield a random chance for fruit to be obtained, which includes Coconuts, rarely.
