The Advanced Supplies Workbench is the final tier of supplies workbench in-game, and allows the player to craft more advanced items and consumables.

Crafting[]
To craft the Advanced Supplies Workbench the player must have unlocked the Advanced Tools memetic, this is only possible once the scenario has reached phase 2: Tame the Staroid. To unlocked the Advanced Tools memetic node press 'O' to open the memetics screen, go to the second tab at the top labled Crafting, and scroll to the final row titled 05 Enlightenment. The memetic node costs 7 ciphers and  5000body[data-theme="light"] .mw-parser-output .energy-links-icon{filter:drop-shadow(0 0 0.125em black)}, granted the player has unlocked the prerequisite node: HP Recovery II.
