I'll Take the Night Shift can be found outside the shed at 6765,-4810 in Aiden's Hideout, Dayton Wetlands.

The "Can Opener" Alan, Marauder
Glower Harbor, Northeast Dayton Wetlands
May 21, ▮▮32
What were my exact words? I told you NOT, under any circumstances, hand over that map we found to the big guy! But did you listen? No! And now all three of us are being "promoted"! I don't know about you, but I don't consider being posted to some godforsaken craphole on an impossible quest to find some long-lost Rosetta treasure a promotion!
