Summary[]
Cheer Up, Trent is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Cheer Up, Trent can be found on the roof of the hospital at 5796,-4496 in Dayton Hospital, Dayton Wetlands.

Cheer Up, Trent[]
Trent Palmieri, the Rosetta Physician
Dayton Hospital, North Dayton Wetlands
April 11, ▮▮18
I thought that applying for an assignment on Nalcott Island would help me develop a more positive attitude towards life, but it seems I was mistaken.
How can Rosetta be expected to think about the welfare of the general population, when it clearly doesn't even give a damn about its own staff? We built that hospital, supposedly in the name of "improving healthcare for the people of Nalcott," but the economy of this region is a complete mess. How may locals can afford our health insurance prices? At our prices, simple cataract surgery would be enough to bankrupt an entire family.
Admit it, Molly, this is just another publicity stunt. All trying to prove that Rosetta, the largest multinational on planet Earth, is a socially conscious enterprise... And as for me? My job is to "spring into action" and rescue Rosetta workers who sustain in juries during the Monolith's construction phase.
But when you get right down to it, all I can really do is a quick examination of the employee's remains (and sometimes, there's not even a body), issue a death certificate, and send it to HR.
