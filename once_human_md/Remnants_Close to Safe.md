Summary[]
Close to Safe is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Close to Safe can be found in the apartment above the diner at 6782,-5782 in Overlook Town, Dayton Wetlands.

Close to Safe[]
Kaitlin March, Mayfly
Overlook Town, South Dayton Wetlands
April 17, ▮▮37
Tracking traces of Deviation led us to the open-air cinema in Overlook Town. It is uncertain whether this Deviation possesses intelligence, but it is evident that it has the ability to actively escape, having already undergone three positional changes. What's perplexing is that this Deviation is extremely persistent in holding "duty screenings," like it considers itself the guardian of this open-air cinema. Even when I tracked it, it stubbornly continued screening (though the content was chaotic).
I have classified it as Safe Level, essentially harmless. I will attempt to search for it once more, but if it cannot be found, so be it. Elliot will definitely mock me, but a Deviation that can elude people should be considered "intelligent" to some extent... According to the First Code, I should let it be.
