Obtaining[]
Crafting[]
Rawhide can be crafted at all three primary, intermediate, and advanced supplies workbenches, granted the player has unlocked the memetic node: Leatherworking, from the Crafting page in the top right, costing 1 cipher. To craft rawhide the player can use any of the following 27 hides/skins/furs in a 1:1 ratio:

Disassembly[]
Rawhide is returned upon disassembly of some crafted items that require Rawhide, as well as a few items found while exploring the wilderness.
