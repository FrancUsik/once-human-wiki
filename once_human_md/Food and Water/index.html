    Basics[]

    Food and drinks are consumables required for the player's character to perform at their best. Food and drinks replenish "Hydration" and/or "Energy", and sometimes, "Sanity" bars when consumed. The first two bars decrease on their own with some time (except for cases mentioned down below), while Sanity will deplete in some places, or after being attacked by some enemies. Some food may give buffs depending on the ingredient used and can be buffed using certain Memetic Specializations.

    Energy[] Appearance of buff "Filled up"
    The bar "Energy" represents saturation of the player. Depending on the value, one can have buffs or debuffs correspondingly, that will change immediately as value of parameter change. There are 4 possible cases:

    60%~100%: HP +400; Melee Weapon DMG +10%; No natural Energy loss for 20 mins when Hydration is maxed out. 30%~60%: HP +200. 0%~30%: Load -10. 0%: Load -20. Hydration[] The bar "Hydration" represents the degree of hydration of the player. Hydration depletes faster than Energy. As with food, depending on the value, players can have buffs or debuffs correspondingly, that will change as the value of the parameter changes. There are 4 possible cases:

    60%-100%: Sprint Speed +20%; Sprint Stamina Cost -50%; No natural Hydration loss for 20 minutes when hydration is maxed out. 30%-60%: Sprint Speed +10%. 0%-30%: Stamina Recovery Speed -50%; Movement Speed -10%. 0%: Stamina Recovery Speed -70%; Movement Speed -20%. There are plenty of different types of food and drink which restore Sanity, Energy, HP, or provide buffs. Most foods can be crafted using a Stove, Electric Stove, or Kitchen Set.
