Summary[]
Saving One Bullet is a piece of Lore found under the Regional Records section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Saving One Bullet can be found in a small group of buildings across the road from the Sutherland Family Orchard at 7450,-5491. Use Spacetime [Q] to reveal.

Saving One Bullet[]
John O'Sullivan: Is it time to retreat?
Joan Sutherland: You go ahead...
John O'Sullivan: You aren't coming? You saw how they fired on that crowd on the bridge. You can't get back onto the island now, even if your last name is Sutherland.
Joan Sutherland: I know that. I'm not staying because I want to try and sneak back onto Mola Island.
John O'Sullivan: So, what? Are you trying to repent? Redeem yourself for causing all this?
Joan Sutherland: Terrible mistakes were made, and there's nothing I can do to undo them. I don't want to run. I'm ready to die.
John O'Sullivan: Suit yourself. remember to leave the last bullet for yourself.
Joan Sutherland: I know. Goodbye, John.
