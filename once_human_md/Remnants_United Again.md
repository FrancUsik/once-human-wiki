United Again[]
Simon Meyer, Head of a Survivor Settlement
Harborside, Southeast Broken Delta
October 7, ▮▮26
There's no one left alive in this place. Everyone's either been devoured by Deviants, or infected by Stardust and driven insane.
I never understood why Denise Cooper was so intent on exploring this place. She said she wanted to scope out the geography of this region for her so-called "Union." It's been a decade since the Starfall first hit, and she wants to find out how many people are still alive, and reach out to them.
Every detail does in that geographic report she's been working on, even an old ruin like this place. When we get back to Oil Well No. 17, (according to her, the "Union" has established some kind of town around that oil well, known as Blackfell), she'll file her report.
The woman is crazy, no doubt. But then, I guess I'm not much better. Otherwise, why would I have accompanied her here?
She's constantly trying to recruit us for her beloved Union. It's all crap. Only an idiot would believe that humanity could be reunited now...
