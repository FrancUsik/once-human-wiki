Keep Your Mind on the Job is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Keep Your Mind on the Job can be found inside the grocery store at 6717,-5695 in Overlook Town, Dayton Wetlands.

Keep Your Mind on the Job[]
Ayas Every, Contractor
Overlook Town, South Dayton Wetlands
May 5, ▮▮17
To be honest, I don't know what the hell you're worried about. We're paid to be engineers, not accountants. The cost of materials, the cost of labor, the cost of transportation, whether the numbers look right or wrong... None of that is any concern of ours.
If Mr. Sutherland wants a town that can compete with those old villas out on Mola Island, then heck, we'll build it for him! He's practically drowning us in cold, hard cash, after all.
Do you get it now? Nothing else matters. Just make sure we stay on schedule for the next quarter, alright? Also, don't forget to check the stuff in the underground food storage at the supermarket. Got it?
