Obtaining[]
Morels can not be gathered from the wilderness in plant form, but instead can be found by looting Storage Crates in level 40+ locations. They can still be grown by planting Morel Seeds. The Fetch-A-Lot Bunny, if this deviation is placed inside an Isolated Securement Unit in the player's territory, can gather Morels and Morel seeds randomly.

Planting[]
Morel Seeds can be planted in Loamy Soil or Planter Boxes, they take 6 hours to mature and need an irrigation level between 50 - 90, and a light level between 70 - 100. For more information on how to cultivate this plant, see the page Seeds and Agriculture.

Deviated Morel[]
Deviated versions of this plant can not be found randomly in the wild, but deviated Morels and Morel seeds cab be found when looting Storage Crates in level 40+ locations. Deviated versions of plants can be grown randomly in Loamy Soil or Planter Boxes, for more information on how to increase the chances of growing deviated plants see the page Seeds and Agriculture.
