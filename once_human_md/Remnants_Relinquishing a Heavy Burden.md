Relinquishing a Heavy Burden can be found on the bench outside the house at 5119,-6651 in Sunbury Farm, Broken Delta.

Relinquishing a Heavy Burden[]
Rebecca Blunt, Farm Owner
Sunbury Farm, South Broken Delta
April 1, ▮▮25
The author of this document is Rebecca Blunt. I own the property rights to Sunbury Farm and the surrounding area. I was also the leader of this shelter in the last years of my life.
As of today, I relinquish this role, and all associated responsibilities and duties.
I have done everything in my power to lead and protect this shelter and the people who gathered here in the aftermath of the disaster. I tried to help the people here embrace their fate with dignity. When there was no alternative, I also helped relieve their pain. I mourn these losses, but I have a clear conscience.
I am the sole remaining survivor in this shelter, and I am about to die. I've got my gun right next to my, still loaded. But I won't kill myself.
To the person who finds this document, I hereby give you everything. This farmhouse, the surrounding land and property, and anything else you can find. It's a gift. No strings attached. Congratulations - you've managed to survive for longer than any of us.
