Summary[]
The Stove is a Production Processing Facility used to craft several types of consumable items.

How To Unlock[]
The Stove is the first Memetic available under the Logistics section. It costs 1 Meme Point (Cipher) and no Energy Link points.
Unlocking this Memetic grants the formula for craft Stove, Salt, Sugar, among other consumables.
