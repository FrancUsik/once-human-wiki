Alloy pickaxes are the next tier of pickaxe after steel pickaxes, and unlock the ability for players to mine tungsten ore. Alloy pickaxes also have higher efficiency allowing the player to mine nodes faster and gather more materials when doing so.

Crafting[]
Crafting an alloy pickaxe requires the player to unlock the memetic node: alloy pickaxes (7 cipher,  2000), meaning they can only be obtained during or after Phase 2: Tame the Staroid. Alloy pickaxes can only be crafted at an intermediate or advanced supplies workbench.
