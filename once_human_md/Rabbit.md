Summary[]
Rabbits are passive animals that can be slain for Rabbit Meat, or captured for animal breeding. Rabbits do not give off any byproducts. For more information on byproducts, see article: Ranching

Description[]
As small and defenseless prey animals, rabbits have evolved to be cautious and quick in order to evade predators effectively.

Location[]
Rabbits are extremely commonly in the Dayton Wetlands, Broken Delta, Iron River, and Chalk Peak &#91;1&#93;.

How to Capture[]
[See: Ranching]

Taming[]
Once a rabbit has been captured, Players can place them inside their territory, along with an animal trough that has food and water inside it. Food and water may also be discarded on the ground. Rabbits have a diet of fruits, vegetables and fodder; their favorite foods consist of herbs, such as sage and honeysuckle.
Tameness increases as the animal's hydration and hunger meters increase above 30%. Their tameness will decrease if their hydration and hunger meters reach 0%.
If slaughtered, rabbits will provide the Player with Rabbit Meat and Fur.

Trivia[]
Lethal Rabbit may spawn after killing a high number of rabbits.
