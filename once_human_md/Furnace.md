Summary[]
The Tier I furnace is used to create Charcoal and smelt Tier I ingots and materials.

How To Get[]
The memetic for the Furnace is automatically unlocked when Players first enter the world. The memetic node, Furnace, can be found in the Cradle under the Infrastructure  page, 01 Awakening.
