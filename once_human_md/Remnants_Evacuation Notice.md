Summary[]
Evacuation Notice is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Evacuation Notice can be found in the reception of the motel at 6619,-5817 in Overlook Town, Dayton Wetlands.

Evacuation Notice[]
Dr. Balla, Sit-86 Op Chief
Overlook Town, South Dayton Wetlands
July 23, ▮▮19
The adverse Effects of this incident will soon be over. Until the lockdown is lifted, all personnel are requested to remain inside their dorms wherever possible. Rosetta security forces will ensure everyone's safety.
Please Note: Emergency food rations will arrive at the camp and be distributed by members of staff in due course.
If you re taken ill, please proceed to the designated medical area immediately, where medical staff can provide a full physical examination and prescribe treatment. Before you enter the dorm, be sure to leave your weapons and gear outside. Thanks for you cooperation.
