    Summary[] Brookham is a settlement located in the Dayton Wetlands at 6405,-4627.

    History[] Brookham is the largest settlement in the Dayton Wetlands. It used to have a population of over two thousand, with a bustling main street running through it. At one time, it was possible to buy pretty much anything on that street, even some quite specialty items. Business hours weren't restricted either, and many stores were open all night long [1].
