Blue Tea[]
Rod Burns, Local Resident
Throughvill, Middle Dayton Wetlands
February 11, ▮▮19
I made a cup of tea in the sunroom next to the fields in the morning, genuine Ceylon Black Tea. But guess what? It came out blue! At first I though my son was tying to play some ridiculous practical joke. I was just getting ready to teach him a lesson he wouldn't soon forget, when I realized that the taste, and even the aroma, were just like they should be.It didn't seem to be contaminated or adulterated with any other substance. I brewed another cup, and - same result!
I had no choice but to throw the whole bag away. And it cost me a lot of money, too - worth far more than any of the supplies in this sunroom! From tomorrow on I'll have to switch to coffee. The next batch of tea isn't due until September.
