# Rustic Set (Armor)

All pieces of the Rustic Set armor are of normal rarity. Having 4 of the 5 pieces of the Rustic Set equipped increases the players Gathering speed by +20%.
The Rustic Set consists of:

Rustic Jacket
Rustic Shoes
Rustic Pants
Rustic Hat
Rustic Gloves
