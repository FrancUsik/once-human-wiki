Han Fei, Survivor
Sunburn Farm, South Broken Delta

Yinxi doesn't know her mother has passed, but Jintan must have heard the gunshot. She's the third one this month. One this kind of thing starts picking up momentum, it's hard to stop.
I'm actually a little envious of these people. At least they get to enjoy one last full meal. And it's not such a bad way to go. Quick, clean. I'd rather die that way then turn into a monster, like Margery and Antonio.

And with two little girls in two... I'm sorry, but it just wouldn't be wise.
