	How To Get


	
	LootedCrafted:Kitchen Set





Bread with Jam can be crafted after the player has found Hawthorn Berries and Wheat, which in Manibus can be found easily by exploring the wilderness. However, players in the Way of Winter scenario that wish to snack on this sweet treat must turn to other means of gathering, such as growing the berries and wheat from seeds, or upgrading a Thermal Tower. For more information on gathering crops via thermal towers, click here.

Obtaining[]
Bread with Jam can be crafted at a Stove or a Kitchen Set by combining 1x Hawthorn Berry and 2x Wheat.
