Obtaining[]
Barreled Premium Fuel can be obtained in several different ways, one is by refining Portable Mixed Fuel at a Refining Facility. At small refinery facilities at a rate of 50:1 every hour, or at a large refinery facilities at a rate of 25:1 every 30 minutes. For the player to be able to use the large refinery facility they must have unlocked the Memetic Specialization: Large Refinery Facility.
Secondly, there is a chance to obtain Barreled Premium Fuel as a reward for successfully completing workshop defenses.
Thirdly, Barreled Premium Fuel can be obtained in small quantities (3x, 6x, 9x, etc) from the crates rewarded to the player for completion of the event: Ancient One's Trial.
Finally, Barreled Premium Fuel can be bought from Nisa for 20 Sproutlets each.

How To Use[]
It can be used to re-fuel vehicles: Barreled Premium Fuel gives 1000 fuel to a vehicle, but from an efficiency standpoint, it's better to use Portable Mixed Fuel.
It's used in the crafting of some items.
