Spawning Bed is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Spawning Bed can be found in the building at 6617,-5166  in Inspection Point 7802, Dayton Wetlands.

Spawning Bed[]
Victor Hammett, Mayfly
Inspection Point 7804, Middle Dayton Wetlands
August 11, ▮▮37
Project BL085Z was created as a backup for project BL082W, and was launched at least 1 year later.
By the time of the Starfall, project completion was lagging at under 70%, but most of the required infrastructure and equipment was already in place. After accessing the observatory's computer, Victor was able to confirm that BL085Z had been successfully run at least once, and was able to calibrate some basic data points related to the "Silver Gate".
As with other Monoliths, BL085Z has been abandoned for nearly three decades. This place would make a perfect "spawning ground," even more so than other Monoliths... The larvae... are starting to hatch.
