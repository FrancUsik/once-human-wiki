Obtaining[]
Beryllium Crystals can be obtained by disassembling junk items found in level 11-20 areas. These areas are open during the first phase of all scenarios.
Players may also come across Crystal Transformation a Memetic Specialization that can be randomly rolled as one of specializations the player receives at levels 40, 45, or 50. This specialization allows the player to craft Beryllium Crystals using x15 Stardust Source and x5 Bronze Ingots at an Electric Furnace.

Uses[]
Beryllium Crystals are essential crafting ingredients in crafting any tier II armor pieces, Weapons, or tools.
