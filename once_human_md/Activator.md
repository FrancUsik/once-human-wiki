Activators are first tier of activator, they act as a therapeutic and are important for regenerating the player's health in a moment of need. To unlock the ability to craft Activators the player must first unlock the memetic node: HP Recovery I (2 cipher,  0) in the Crafting section of the memetics page, granted they have unlocked the prerequisite node: Essential Tools.

Crafting[]
This item can randomly drop from some mobs and can occasionally be found in crates, however players may find it more useful to craft Activators. They can be crafted at any Supplies Workbench. There are two formulas that produce the same result, one using Boiled Water and the other using Purified Water.
