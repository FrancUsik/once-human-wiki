Location[]
A Heroic Deed can be found in the house at 6033,-6914 in Harborside, Broken Delta.

A Heroic Deed[]
August Payne, Barista
Harborside, Southeast Broken Delta
August 3, ▮▮19
Two hours ago, there was a breach in the eastern blockade. Luckily, it was blocked in time, but at the cost of two lives, Connell and Kelly. Kelly was Eponine's sister, and she can't stop crying. I should have hugged her, comforted her, but damn it... even at the end of the world, I'm still a hopeless coward.
Oddly enough, Harris just brought me half a bottle of liquor and praised my... bravery? He said if I hadn't stepped up and charged the monsters, firing a round, everyone would've been done for it. But I don't even remember doing anything so heroic... Well, there are quite a few blue stains on me, maybe I did do something in the hear of the moment... Anyways, I really need this half bottle of liquor...
