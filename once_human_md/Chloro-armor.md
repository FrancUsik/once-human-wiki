Summary[]
Chloro-armor is an elixer that grants Chloro-armor (Whim) upon use. When health drops to critical levels (below 20%) the Chloro-armor Status Effect is applied, restoring all health and sanity to 100%. Even after activation, the Chloro-armor (Whim) remains, however, the Chloro-armor Status Effect has a 10 minute cooldown (will only apply once every 10 minutes for the duration of the Whim.)

How To Get[]
To unlock the formula for Chloro-armor you must first secure Harveseed in an Isolated Securement Unit.

Tips[]
The Heart Vine material is obtained from a Harveseed placed inside an Isolated Securement Unit.
The Chloro-armor (Whim) is applied upon item use, it will last for 7200s (2 hours.)
The Chloro-armor Status Effect is activated once HP drops below 20%.
The Chloro-armor Status Effect can only be applied 10 minutes after it was last applied.
Note that the description states 'recover' and not 'restore' for HP, this means that any sanity that is currently missing will also be recovered.
