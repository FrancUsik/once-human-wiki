Summary[]
Treasure Land is a piece of Lore found under the Remnants section of Field Guide.

Location[]
Treasure Land can be found on the second floor of the building at 6648,-5172 in Inspection Point 7802, Dayton Wetlands.

Treasure Land[]
Kaitlin March, Mayfly
Inspection Point 7804, Middle Dayton Wetlands
June 19, ▮▮38
Due to the scarcity of safe places to store money (obviously, it's the apocalypse), I've buried my savings. I'm not sure if I'll come back alive after this mission, so I'm leaving his note behind. If you happen to a fortunate individual who reaches the western power station (at the other end of Throughville), you can find a box buried in shallow soil next to the outdoor garbage heap. If I perish, consider this wealth as a gift to you. However, if I'm fortunate enough to return, your efforts may be in vain. It all depends on your luck.
